This is the core of the playbook: 13 competency areas that together describe what a DevOps professional actually operates, builds, and is accountable for in 2026. They're grouped into four clusters that follow the shape of a real delivery organization — from shipping code, to running the platform underneath it, to keeping it reliable and secure, to the emerging and human-side skills that separate senior engineers from principal ones.

Each competency page follows the same structure: **why it matters now**, **core skills & tools**, **what you must have operated**, **evidence you can show**, **KPIs**, a **maturity table**, and **ready-to-use proof statements** for resumes and interviews.
